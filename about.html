Somatics has been my passion since 1998, when I stumbled upon Body Awareness courses at Western WA University’s Fairhaven College. Thomas Hanna defined somatics as “…the field of study dealing with somatic phenomena, i.e., the human being as experienced by himself or herself from the inside.” Studying about the history of the body, various types of bodywork, and experiencing being more embodied myself, changed my life. I attended Brenneke School of Massage in 2000 and have continued on this path with curiosity and excitement. It feels good to be in my body and help others feel better too.
