We are living in challenging times. There are plenty of opportunities for nervous system activation in today’s world, and not enough for deactivation. Our natural cycles are often interrupted, and this can increase stress and symptoms, or make us feel disconnected from our bodies. Taking time for regular deactivation is essential for our nervous systems to reset and regulate. While we can do some of this on our own, we are social beings designed to co-regulate with others, so having help with this can be very therapeutic.

As you feel less mental and physical stress, your body’s healing wisdom emerges and develops. I love supporting clients in this process.

Massage

Massage and bodywork can be an important part of overall wellness and happiness. We’re wired for touch and it’s a basic human need.

Our massage sessions will most likely be a combination of attention to specific areas, and to the nervous system as a whole. It is a varied sensory experience where you are taken care of.

My influences are Myofascial Release, Trager ™, Swedish, positional release, compress/decompress, active muscle resistance, Esalen ™, Presencing ™ and more.

My touch can be from gentle to firm, depending on your preferences. (I don't do super deep tissue or exert a lot of heavy pressure.)

I listen and work with you and your body so your body can integrate the experience and function better over time.

Sessions can be clothed, or unclothed with professional draping.

Clients say:

“It’s a good balance of relaxing and therapeutic.”

Somatic Support

In this work, I help you access and grow your unique physiological resources to relax, reconnect, and feel better. I bring spaciousness, curiosity and a stable presence to support your nervous system in increasing its capacity and resilience.

This can be helpful for pain, illness/injury, stress, or trauma--or just feeling more connected to your body and self.

We may include gentle touch, conversation/somatic inquiry, exploration of the external environment, or other sensory activities suited to you. These experiences gradually rewire the brain and remap the body.

My influences are Somatic Experiencing ™, Organic Intelligence ™, and Transforming the Experience-Based Brain ™.

Sessions are clothed, and either seated in a chair, or on a massage table.
