Covid Precautions

I received the 2nd dose of the Moderna Covid vaccine 2/9/21. I am seeing only fully vaccinated clients at this time. Masks are legally required for both of us during sessions; massage therapists are licensed healthcare providers and anywhere we practice massage is considered a healthcare setting.

Most clients say they’re surprised the mask doesn’t bother them and they forget about it in a few minutes. If you do have any issues when face down in the cradle for massage sessions, just let me know and we will try to find a way for you to be comfortable.

We can do more or all of the massage in the sidelying position (this is a bit different if you’ve never tried it, but it's used during pregnancy and for other reasons, and some folks love it) and/or face up (I can access many areas on the back side of the body by reaching underneath when clients are face up).

Additional Precautions

  • Air filters running for increased ventilation.
  • Monitoring self for temperature and symptoms, and screening clients.
  • Following masking and social distancing guidelines in my personal life as well.
  • Implementiion of enhanced sanitation between each session.

Massage

Massage and bodywork can be a great part of overall wellness and happiness. We’re wired for touch and it’s a basic human need.

Our massage sessions will most likely be a combination of attention to specific areas, and to the nervous system as a whole. It is a varied sensory experience where you are taken care of.

My influences are Myofascial Release, Trager ™, Swedish, positional release, compress/decompress, active muscle resistance, Esalen ™, Presencing ™ and more.

My touch can be from gentle to firm, depending on your preferences. I have moved away from heavy “deep tissue” work. When we are less forceful, we can have a different somatic conversation and your body is encouraged to respond in a way that is more integrated and lasts better.

Sessions can be clothed, or unclothed with draping.

Somatic Support

In this work, I help you access and grow your unique physiological resources to relax, reconnect, and feel better. I bring spaciousness, curiosity and a stable presence to support your nervous system in increasing its capacity and resilience.

We may include conversation/somatic inquiry, gentle touch, exploration of the external environment, or other sensory activities suited to you.

My influences are Somatic Experiencing ™, Organic Intelligence ™, and Transforming Touch ™.

Sessions are clothed, and either seated in a chair or on a massage table.

Sessions can be clothed, or unclothed with draping.
